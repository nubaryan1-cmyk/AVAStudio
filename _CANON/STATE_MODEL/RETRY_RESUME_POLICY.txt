AVA — RETRY & RESUME POLICY
Version: 2.0 (FINAL)
Status: LOCKED — IMMUTABLE
Authority: OWNER DECISION (2025-01-11)

RETRY POLICY:

ALLOWED IF:
- state = FAILED
- reason IN:
  GPU_OOM
  EXECUTOR_ERROR
  TIMEOUT_ERROR
  SYSTEM_ERROR

FORBIDDEN IF:
- reason IN:
  CONFIG_ERROR
  DATASET_ERROR
  USER_ACTION

RETRY MECHANISM:
- FAILED jobs are re-queued to IN_QUEUE (with retries counter incremented)
- NOTE: RETRYING state is PERMANENTLY FORBIDDEN — use IN_QUEUE for retry

LIMITS:
- max_retry = 3
- retry_delay = exponential (1m, 5m, 15m)

RESUME POLICY:
- NOTE: PAUSED state is PERMANENTLY FORBIDDEN
- Resume functionality is NOT supported

CANCEL POLICY:
- CANCELLED is terminal
- no retry
- no resume

RULES:
- Policies are enforced by Job Core
- Executors must not override policy
- PAUSED and RETRYING states are PERMANENTLY FORBIDDEN
